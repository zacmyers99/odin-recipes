Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a recipe about chocolate chip cookies. My grandmother used to make chocolate chip cookies. She died last year, but that's okay. We don't mind. Today we're going to make chocolate chip cookies with chocolate chips, sugar, and flour.

BChocolate chip cookies have always been one of my favorite dishes. Making chocolate chip cookies can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Probably some salt in there too
  • A bunch of other stuff but I understand the exercise at this point

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix everything together
  3. Throw it in the oven for an hour
  4. Slather your tastebuds with deliciousness
