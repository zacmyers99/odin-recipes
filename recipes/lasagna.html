Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe about lasagna. My grandmother used to make lasagna. She died last year, but that's okay. We don't mind. Today we're going to make lasagna with cheese and meat.

Lasagna has always been one of my favorite dishes. Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • A bunch of other stuff but I understand the exercise at this point

Directions

  1. Cook sausage
  2. Stir stuff together with tomato sauce
  3. Cook noodles
  4. Slap it all together
