Banana Bread

Description

This is a recipe about banana bread. My grandmother used to make banana bread. She died last year, but that's okay. We don't mind. Today we're going to make banana bread with bananas, sugar and flour.

Banana bread has always been one of my favorite dishes. Making banana bread can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour/li>
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups mashed overripe bananas
  • A bunch of other stuff but I understand the exercise at this point

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix everything together
  3. Throw it in the oven for an hour
  4. Slather your tastebuds with deliciousness
